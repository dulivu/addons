Register and control of bookings in sale orders

Manage bookings and sales from the same window

Sales

Bookings automatically generate sale lines based on time measurement units.
You only configure your services with a single unit price per unit of time.
You can configure the module so that each sales line records fractions of time or complete units.

Calendar

Control your bookings on the calendar and filter them at your convenience.
Default filters are already set: booking to enter, booking to leave, unconfirmed bookings, etc.
You can modify the date of unconfirmed bookings, or create new one from the calendar directly.

Configurations

- You can round or not the time booked, config the round configurations.
- You can establish that the booking must be registered a partner.
